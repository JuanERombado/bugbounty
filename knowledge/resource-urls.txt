## Strategy Prompts To Derive From These Sources

Accounting drift:
Find where two ledgers can temporarily disagree, then ask who can profit before reconciliation.

State-machine edge:
Find transitions that are safe alone but unsafe when paused, delayed, retried, canceled, or partially completed.

Authorization confusion:
Find places where caller, signer, owner, delegate, operator, messenger, and receiver are treated as equivalent.

Cross-chain assumption:
Find where a local contract trusts a remote message, replay state, retry path, bridge alias, or finalization status.

Novelty gate:
For every lead, search root cause terms and existing tests before writing a full PoC.
